About the Venue

Intimate Weddings,
Within Your Reach

San Antonio's first intimate outdoor garden wedding venue — where ancient oaks, manicured gardens, and a peaceful setting come together for your most important day.

Our Philosophy

Small, Beautiful
and Affordable

The Gardens at West Green was the first venue in San Antonio to offer a genuine alternative to the traditional event hall. Our peaceful, serene two-and-a-half acres — surrounded by centurion oaks and natural vegetation — provide a stunning backdrop for your wedding day.

We specialize in intimate, personalized events. Whether you're planning an elopement for two or a garden reception for eighty, your day should feel uniquely yours — not templated. We give you a beautiful blank canvas and the flexibility to make it your own.
