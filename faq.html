The Venue

What is your maximum capacity?

Our maximum capacity is 80 people. All packages include up to 60 guests, with additional guests available at $15 per person up to the maximum.

Do you have onsite parking?

Yes — our onsite parking easily accommodates up to 52 vehicles. Additional parking at the back of the property is also available for vendors.

Will The Gardens still be green for a winter wedding?

Yes! The Gardens at West Green is composed of evergreen trees and shrubs, so it looks beautiful all year round — no matter the season.

Do I need an appointment to tour?

Yes, tours are by appointment. We can schedule as late as 6:30 pm Monday through Thursday. Friday, Saturday, and Sunday appointments are based on availability and are scheduled around events already on the calendar.

Schedule via Contact page

What other events do you host besides weddings?

We only host weddings. This means our full attention and experience is always focused on making your wedding day perfect.

Food & Drink

Do you allow outside catering?

Yes — you are welcome to bring in the caterer of your choice. We want your menu to reflect your vision, not ours.

Can I bring my own drinks and alcoholic beverages?

Yes, you may bring in your own drinks and alcoholic beverages. However, we do require that you use a TABC licensed and insured bartending service from our vendor list to serve your guests.

See our Vendor List for approved bartenders

Are you pet friendly?

Yes, we love your fur babies! Best practice is to have them participate in the ceremony, then be taken home by a designated pet sitter for the reception.

Booking & Payment

What is required to reserve my wedding date?

A deposit of 50% of your base package price is required to reserve your date. This payment may be split into two consecutive monthly payments.

When is the final balance due?

The final balance of your base package price is due 4 weeks prior to your event.

When is my final guest count due?

Your final guest count is due 2 weeks before your event. Any guests over your package's included count will be billed at that time.

Can I reserve and pay my deposit online?

Yes! We can send an electronic contract and invoice directly to your email address. Once the document is signed and payment is submitted, your date is officially reserved.

Fully paperless & easy

Day-of Details

How much time is included in my package?

Most packages include approximately 8 hours total: up to 3 hours of early arrival for setup, 4–6 hours of event time (depending on your package), and 1 hour after the reception to gather personal belongings and depart.

Can I add additional hours?

Yes. You may add pre-arrival hours at $100 per hour and additional event hours at $495 per hour. A half-hour extension is also available for $250 (Mon–Thu package).

What is included in my rental package?

Your package includes: all chairs for the ceremony and reception, 60-inch round dining tables, linens, sign-in table, cake table, sweetheart table, gift table, wooden arch or floral candelabras, bar table area, easels, and two dressing rooms — plus full setup of everything we provide.

Vendors & Music

Can I bring in my own vendors?

Yes! You may bring in a vendor of your preference for most services — except the bartending service and DJ, which must come from our vetted vendor list.

Can I hire my own DJ?

We require that you hire a DJ from our Vendor List. We carefully vet every DJ on the list to ensure a professional, worry-free event at a reasonable price. We're always adding new DJs, so check back often.

DJ must be from our vendor list

Do you allow live bands?

We do not allow full bands. However, you are absolutely welcome to hire acoustical ensembles, string ensembles, mariachis, pianists, and guitarists — they all create a beautiful atmosphere for ceremonies and cocktail hours.
