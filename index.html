About the Venue

A Garden Wedding Like No Other

The Gardens at West Green is San Antonio's most unique small wedding venue. We specialize in outdoor garden weddings for the couple who is looking for an intimate ceremony and reception venue at an affordable price.

Nestled beneath ancient live oak trees, our grounds feature a grand fountain, white-columned pergola, manicured hedges, and string lights that create an atmosphere unlike any other venue in the city.
